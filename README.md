## Research Overview

This repository contains the full research artifacts of the doctoral thesis
developed within the PPGC/UFPel. The research investigates the impact of
modern video compression codecs — primarily **AV1** compared to **AVC/H.264** —
on the performance of **Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)** applied to
video-based tasks.

The central hypothesis is that codec-induced compression artifacts affect
feature extraction in deep learning pipelines, and that understanding this
relationship can inform better trade-offs between storage/bandwidth efficiency
and model accuracy in real-world deployed systems.
